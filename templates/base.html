How To Play!

HOW TO PLAY

WELCOME TO THE GEOGRAPHY QUIZ

To play to game follow these instructions

1.   Select a CATEGORY from the options below by clicking on the image.
2.   Enter your USERNAME before starting the quiz.
3.   Go through each multiple choice question and select your ANSWER.
4.   You only get ONE CHANCE per question.
5.   If you answer INCORRECTLY, the correct answer will be displayed.
6.   When the game has FINISHED you can see your final score.
